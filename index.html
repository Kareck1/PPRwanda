Hvem er vi?

P&P Rwanda Rejser blev etableret i 2020 af fætrene Patrick Ilunga og Mazimpaka Phillippe. Ideen til atstarte rejsebureauet udsprang af Patricks mange rejser til Rwanda, hvor han introducerede danskevenner, familie og bekendte til sit hjemlandets kultur og natur. De unikke og mindeværdige oplevelser,som hans gæster fik, førte til ideen om at skabe et rejsebureau, der kunne tilbyde disse ekstraordinære rejser til alle interesserede danskere.

Det, der adskiller P&P Rwanda Tours fra andre rejsebureauer til Afrika er den eksklusive adgang tilsteder, som ofte er ukendte for almindelige turister. Dette kan kun lade sig gøre takket være Phillippesomfattende lokalkendskab og hans mange års erfaring i Rwandas turisme-og hotelindustri.

Hos P&P Rwanda Tours får dusom rejsende det bedste fra begge verdener, dvs. en dansk-og kinyarwanda-talende rejseleder i form af Patrick og Phillippe. Gennem Phillippes netværk og ekspertise sikrer vi dig en autentisk og uforglemmelig rejseoplevelse. Vi har en række rejsepakker som henvender sig til singlerejser, familierejser, studieture og grupperejser.

Vores grundlæggende vision er, at Rwanda bliver en af danskernes foretrukne rejsedestinationer iAfrika. Som et af de sikreste lande i Afrika, kan du trygt færdes i landets gaderuden at bekymre dig omdin sikkerhed. Rwanda er enorm rigdom på kultur og natur, som venter på at blive udforsket af dig. Visørger for, at du som rejsende med os komme tæt på det meste, herunder livet som det leves afrwandere.

Vi ønsker, at du får et andet perspektiv Rwanda og Afrika end det, du får gennem medier, bøger ogrejsereportager. Rwanda er kendt for sin gæstfrihed og bæredygtige turisme, som du bidrager til ved atrejse med os, da vi udelukkende samarbejder med den lokalbefolkning. Hvis du drømmer om enautentiske oplevelse og eventyr i et Afrikansk land, så er Rwanda det rette sted for dig

Vores ambition er, at du får en fantastisk oplevelse, og derfor skræddersyer rejsen, så den passer tildig. Om du er til badeferie, vandreferie, safariferie, storbyferie, madferie eller en blanding af det hele,så har vi redskaberne og netværket til at opfylde dine ønsker. Vi har en lang række af lokalesamarbejdspartnere, såsom hoteller, safariparker, museer, restauranter m.m., som muliggør at P&PRwandaTours kan sikre dig god pris priser.

Why Choose Rwanda as Your Next Travel Destination

Rwanda is an ideal travel destination for anyone seeking a journey beyond the ordinary. The combination of natural wonders, cultural experiences, and relaxation makes Rwanda a unique destination that offers unforgettable memories.
If you dream of safari adventures, Rwanda is home to some of the world’s most iconic wildlife. In Akagera National Park, you can embark on a classic safari with a chance to see the “Big Five” – lions, elephants, rhinos, buffaloes, and leopards – as well as many other fascinating species. For water enthusiasts, a boat trip on Akagera’s largest lake, Lake Ihema, allows you to get up close to hippos, crocodiles, and an impressive array of birdlife. The park also offers fishing opportunities in its lakes, providing a relaxing activity amid stunning natural surroundings.

Birdwatching is another highlight, as Akagera is a haven for bird lovers, boasting an impressive 500 bird species. When night falls, you can venture on a night safari to spot nocturnal animals such as hyenas and leopards, which are harder to see during the day. The park offers various accommodation options, including luxury safari lodges nestled within the park.

But Rwanda is much more than just safaris. The country also offers tranquil beach getaways by the beautiful Lake Kivu, one of Africa’s great lakes, located 150 km from the capital. Here, you can enjoy picturesque views, swim in the lake’s waters, or relax on its beaches. Nature walks in the area allow you to explore the landscape, meet the locals, and discover the rich culinary culture.
For those interested in culture and local lifestyles, a visit to one of Rwanda’s many coffee farms is a must. Renowned for its world-class coffee, Rwanda offers an intimate experience of coffee production, including tastings and interactions with local farmers who proudly showcase their craft. This provides an authentic insight into Rwanda’s rural life.

For city enthusiasts, Kigali, the capital, offers a modern urban experience with an African touch. Known for its cleanliness, safety, and vibrant culture, Kigali is one of Africa’s most dynamic cities. The city is rich in history, with highlights including the Kigali Genocide Memorial, a deeply moving site that delves into the country’s history of colonialism, genocide, and recovery. Kigali also boasts art galleries, markets, and restaurants serving both local and international cuisine. Visitors will find a wide range of accommodations, from boutique hotels to luxury options with pools, where the exceptional Rwandan hospitality ensures a memorable stay.
In summary, Rwanda offers a complete travel experience that blends adventure, relaxation, culture, nature, and history. Whether you dream of safaris, beach holidays, or exploring local culture and a modern city, Rwanda has something for every type of traveler. At P&P Rwanda Tours, we are ready to make your travel dreams a reality.

20 Facts About Rwanda – What You Should Know Before You Travel:

01 Capital

Kigali, known for its safety, cleanliness, and modern infrastructure.

02 Visa

Danish tourists can obtain a visa upon arrival or apply online via Rwanda’s e-visa portal.

03 Languages

Official languages are Kinyarwanda, French, and English, with Swahili also widely spoken.

04 Currency

The local currency is the Rwandan Franc (RWF), though US dollars are widely accepted.

05 Climate

Rwanda has a tropical climate with two rainy seasons (March–May and October–November) but generally mild weather due to its high altitude.

06 Best Travel Time

The dry seasons from June to September and December to February are ideal for visiting.

07 Time Zone

Rwanda operates on Central Africa Time (CET +1 hour).

08 Safety

Rwanda is one of the safest countries in Africa, with a secure environment in cities and national parks.

09 Transport

Travel is easy via taxis, motorcycle taxis (motos), or hiring a car with a driver.

10 Vaccinations

Yellow fever vaccination is mandatory, and malaria protection is recommended.

11 Wildlife

Rwanda is famous for its mountain gorillas, which can be seen in Volcanoes National Park. Gorilla trekking permits must be booked in advance.

12 Safari

Akagera National Park offers classic safaris with the “Big Five” – lions, elephants, rhinos, buffaloes, and leopards.

13 Ecoturism

Rwanda emphasizes sustainable tourism, encouraging visitors to respect the environment and wildlife.

14 Coffee Tours

Coffee farm visits are popular, where you can taste and learn about Rwanda’s premium coffee.

15 Payments

Credit cards are accepted in most larger hotels and restaurants, but carrying cash is advisable in rural areas.

16 Plastic Ban

Rwanda is one of Africa’s cleanest countries due to a strict ban on plastic bags since 2008.

17 Healthcare

Good healthcare facilities are available in Kigali, but they may be limited outside the capital. Travel insurance is essential

18 Kigali Genocide Memorial

This memorial is a must-visit to learn about the country’s history and the 1994 genocide against the Tutsi.

19 Cuisine

Try local dishes such as “brochettes” (grilled skewers), “ibihaza” (pumpkin dishes), and “umutsima” (maize or cassava-based meals).

20 Lake Kivu

A serene destination for relaxation, offering swimming, boat trips, and visits to towns like Gisenyi and Kibuye.
