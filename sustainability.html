Our Commitment to Sustainability

At PPR Wanda Tours, we believe travel should benefit people, places, and the planet.
Our vision connects Rwanda and Denmark through responsible tourism and long-term impact.

Sustainable Tourism Principles

We are dedicated to making every journey positive for the environment and local communities. Sustainability is at the heart of our business in both Rwanda and Denmark.

  • Support Local: We partner with local guides, businesses, and artisans to ensure tourism strengthens communities.
  • Eco-friendly Practices: We minimize waste, reduce our carbon footprint, and encourage green choices on every trip.
  • Respect Nature & Culture: Our tours protect wildlife, preserve heritage, and celebrate the diversity of both countries.
  • Education & Awareness: Guests learn about conservation, culture, and ways to travel responsibly.
  • Continuous Improvement: We regularly review our impact and seek new ways to do better—for people and the planet.
Every journey with us helps fund conservation, community projects, and cross-cultural understanding between Rwanda and Denmark.

How You Can Help

  • Choose eco-friendly options like refillable bottles and ethical souvenirs.
  • Respect local customs and wildlife on every tour.
  • Offset your travel and support community initiatives.
  • Give feedback—your ideas make us better!
