About the Group Tour

Gather your friends, family, or colleagues and explore Rwanda’s stunning landscapes together. This tour offers guided experiences through Akagera National Park, cultural sites, and breathtaking scenic locations.

What’s Included?

  • Guided wildlife safari in Akagera National Park
  • Luxury accommodations for group stays
  • Authentic Rwandan cuisine
  • Team-building activities and cultural experiences
  • Transportation and dedicated tour guide

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome Dinner

Day 2: Akagera Safari - Spot lions, elephants & giraffes

Day 3: Cultural Village Tour & Traditional Dance Show

Day 4: Lake Kivu Relaxation & Boat Cruise
