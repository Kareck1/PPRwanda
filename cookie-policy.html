Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how PPRWANDA Tours ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. This policy applies to all visitors, with particular attention to users from Denmark and Rwanda.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function, remember your preferences, and can be used for analytics or marketing purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Examples
Functional Essential for the website to function properly, e.g., remembering your cookie preferences or login state. Session ID, cookie consent
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. Google Analytics, Matomo
Marketing Used to deliver personalized ads and track campaign effectiveness. Facebook Pixel, Google Ads

Your Consent

On your first visit, and whenever you update your preferences, you will be asked to accept or customize your cookie choices. Functional cookies are always enabled; you can choose whether to accept Analytics and Marketing cookies.

If you are located in Denmark or Rwanda, we comply with local data protection and privacy regulations, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and local laws.

How to Change Your Cookie Preferences

You can update your preferences anytime by clicking the cookie banner or contacting us. You may also clear cookies via your browser settings.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services we use (e.g., analytics providers). Please refer to their privacy policies for more details.
