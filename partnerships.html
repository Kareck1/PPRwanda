Our Partnerships

At PPR Wanda Tours, we believe in the power of collaboration. Our partners help us deliver authentic, memorable, and sustainable travel experiences across Rwanda and beyond.

Featured Partners

Rwanda Development Board

We collaborate closely with the RDB to promote Rwanda’s tourism, conservation, and sustainable economic growth.

Visit Rwanda

Our partnership with Visit Rwanda lets us offer exclusive access and insights into Rwanda’s top destinations and cultural events.

African Wildlife Foundation

Committed to responsible tourism, we support wildlife conservation and community development in partnership with AWF.

RwandAir

Our collaboration with RwandAir ensures seamless travel options and special offers for our tour guests.

UNESCO

We work with UNESCO to protect and promote Rwanda’s world heritage sites and cultural treasures.

World Bank

Supporting sustainable tourism development, our partnership with the World Bank helps empower local communities.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Mutual Growth: Expand opportunities, share expertise, and unlock new markets together.
