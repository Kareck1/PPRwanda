Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how PPR Wanda Tours protects your data and respects your rights as you travel with us in Rwanda, Denmark, or online.

Introduction

PPR Wanda Tours ("PPRW", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number, address
  • Booking and travel preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely, never stored by us directly)
  • Communication history (emails, messages, feedback)
  • Website usage data (cookies, IP address, analytics)
  • Any other information you provide to us voluntarily

How We Use Your Data

  • To manage bookings and provide travel services
  • To communicate with you about your trip or inquiries
  • To personalize your experience and offer relevant services
  • For legal and regulatory compliance
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • For marketing (with your consent)
Note: We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

Sharing Your Information

  • With trusted partners (e.g. hotels, guides, payment processors) only as needed to deliver your booked services
  • With legal authorities if required by law
  • With your explicit consent for any other purpose

Data Security

  • We use secure technologies and restrict access to your information
  • Payment data is processed using industry-standard encryption
  • We regularly review our security policies and practices

Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction: You can update or correct your data
  • Deletion: You can ask us to delete your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection: You may opt out of marketing at any time
  • Restriction: You can limit how we use your data
To exercise your rights, email us at privacy@pprwandatours.com

Cookies & Tracking

  • We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic
  • You can control cookie settings in your browser
  • Some cookies are necessary for site functionality; others are optional

International Transfers

  • Your data may be processed in Rwanda, Denmark, or other countries as needed to provide services
  • We ensure adequate protection for cross-border data transfers

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy periodically; significant changes will be communicated on our website
  • Current version effective: May 2025
Questions? Contact our team at privacy@pprwandatours.com or see our Terms & Conditions.
