Partnering in Action
Conservation in Volcanoes National Park
In partnership with AWF and local communities, we helped fund anti-poaching patrols and education programs, resulting in increased gorilla populations and new eco-tourism jobs.
Rwanda–Denmark Cultural Exchange
Our cross-cultural events with Danish partners fostered creative collaboration, youth workshops, and joint tourism campaigns that reached thousands in both countries.
Women in Tourism
With UNESCO and RDB, we implemented training for women guides and entrepreneurs, increasing female representation and leadership in Rwanda’s tourism sector.
Building Sustainable Lodges
Our eco-lodges, developed with World Bank support, use local materials and solar power, providing jobs and protecting Rwanda’s landscapes.
