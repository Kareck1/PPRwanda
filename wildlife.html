Wildlife & Adventure
Duration: 5 days   |   Destinations: Volcanoes National Park, Akagera National Park, Lake Ihema

Experience Rwanda’s most iconic wildlife and landscapes—with expert guides, premium lodges, and seamless logistics. Get closer to nature than ever before!
Gorilla Trekking
Guided trek to see endangered mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park.
Big 5 Safari
Game drives in Akagera NP—spot lions, elephants, giraffes, hippos and more.
Lake Ihema Cruise
Boat trip for hippos, crocodiles and dazzling birdlife on Lake Ihema.
Nature Hikes
Explore scenic trails and hidden gems with local eco-guides.
Sample Itinerary
  • Day 1: Arrival in Kigali, welcome dinner, transfer to Volcanoes NP.
  • Day 2: Gorilla trekking in Volcanoes NP, cultural village visit.
  • Day 3: Scenic drive to Akagera NP, afternoon game drive, overnight in safari lodge.
  • Day 4: Early morning game drive, Lake Ihema boat cruise for hippos, picnic lunch.
  • Day 5: Optional nature hike, return to Kigali, city tour & markets, departure.
