Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about travel, Rwanda, Denmark, and our services—answered!

FAQ

  • What is PPR Wanda Tours?
    PPR Wanda Tours is a travel company connecting Rwanda and Denmark by offering cultural, adventure, and custom tours. We help travelers experience the best of both countries.
  • Do you have tours in both Denmark and Rwanda?
    Yes! We organize tours and experiences in both Rwanda and Denmark, including cross-cultural trips and exchange programs.
  • How can I book a tour?
    You can contact us via our Contact page or email us at info@pprwandatours.com. Online booking is coming soon!
  • Are your tours eco-friendly?
    Yes, sustainability is a core value. We focus on responsible tourism, support local communities, and minimize environmental impact. Learn more on our Sustainability page.
  • Can you create custom/bespoke tours?
    Absolutely! We love customizing tours to fit your interests, schedule, or group. Just get in touch and tell us your dream trip.
  • How do I get a travel visa for Rwanda or Denmark?
    We can provide guidance and official links, but visa requirements depend on your nationality. Contact us for current advice and help with the process.
  • What forms of payment do you accept?
    We accept bank transfers, Mobile Money (for Rwanda), and will soon accept credit cards and online payments.
  • Are there any current job opportunities?
    At the moment, we do not have open positions. Please check our Careers page or contact us if you’re interested in future opportunities.
  • How can I stay updated on new tours and offers?
    Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter (coming soon!) for the latest news and offers.
  • How do you ensure guest safety?
    We work with licensed guides and providers, follow local regulations, and monitor health and safety guidelines in both Rwanda and Denmark.
