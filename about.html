About PPR Wanda Tours

Connecting Rwanda and Denmark through unique, meaningful travel experiences.

Who We Are

PPR Wanda Tours was founded on the belief that travel has the power to connect people, broaden horizons, and foster deep cultural understanding. With roots in both Rwanda and Denmark, we offer authentic, immersive journeys that reflect the beauty, resilience, and warmth of these two nations.
